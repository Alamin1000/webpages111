Sri Lanka is located at Indian ocean, just off the south east cost of Inia. Shape like a teardrop, the island of Sri Lanka measures about 415 kilometer from north south, and about 220 kilometer from east to west, with a total land area of about 65,600 square kilometer. It has more than 1340 kilometers of coastline.
Despite its modest size Sri Lanka have a population of 20 million. Sri Lanka is a multi-religious, multi-racial and multi- lingual country. The four major ethnic groups are the Sinhalesel, Sri Lankan Tamils, Indian Tamil, Sri Lankas Moor or Muslims. A fifth group, the vehddhas are the original inhabitants of the island.
